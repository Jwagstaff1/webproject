Health Benefits of Resistance Training

Resistance training, also known as strength training or weightlifting, offers a myriad of benefits beyond just bulking up muscles.

Overall Health Benefits

As was mentioned in the previous paragraph, in addition to looking and feeling better, weight training can assist in many other ways. Forbes Health has a web page about these benefits

  • Increased Muscle Size and Strength
  • Improved Cardiovasular Health
  • Increased Bone Density
  • Stabilize and Protect Joints
  • Reduce Body Fat
  • Support Mental Well-Being
  • Improve Sleep Quality
